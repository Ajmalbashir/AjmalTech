I specialize in PHP (Laravel), JavaScript, Vue.js, Blade templating, REST APIs, MySQL, and also handle full-stack projects including admin panels and custom frontend development.

2. Can you fix or update an existing website?

Yes, I can work on existing codebases, fix bugs, add new features, improve speed, or redesign specific sections without disturbing other parts of your site.

I offer both task-based and project-based pricing. For smaller tasks or urgent bug fixes, I work on a half-payment upfront model. Full projects can be priced after discussion depending on scope and timeline.

In most cases, I can begin within 24-48 hours. For urgent tasks, you can reach out directly and I’ll try to accommodate your request as soon as possible.
